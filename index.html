About

Blithe Rocher is an engineering manager based in Alameda, California. She works for Looker and leads the Delivery and Integrations team to deliver data outside of the Looker platform. Prior to becoming a developer, she received a PhD in physical chemistry from the University of Southern California.

When she's not crafting web applications, she spends time watching baseball, reading books, or with her family.
