About

Blithe Rocher is an engineering manager based in Alameda, California. She works for Google and leads engineering teams to help people get the answers they need from their data at Looker. Prior to becoming a developer, she received a PhD in physical chemistry from the University of Southern California.

When she's not crafting web applications, she's watching baseball, reading books, or spending time with her family.
